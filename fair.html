Slavery Awareness Fair

Project Manager: Survivor Communication

As a school we were split into a few groups each tasked with different activities to inform our community about modern day slavery. Choosing to be apart of the survivor communication group and being able to contact hundreds of survivors was a very difficult task, but I was really excited to start. We were tasked with reaching out to trafficking survivors, to broadcast their story at our event and inform our local community on modern day slavery ,which is believed to be a nonexistent issue.

As one of the project managers, I realized the importance of multitasking and deadlines. The two most important components were lost for a few weeks but as a dedicated project manager I felt th at it was my responsibility as well to make sure they were on task. Reflecting on this experience later on taught me the importance of deadlines and multitasking.
