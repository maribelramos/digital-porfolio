Slavery Awareness Fair

Project Manager: Survivor Communication

As a school, students were split into a few groups, each tasked with different activities to inform our community about modern day slavery. Choosing to be a part of the survivor communication group and contacting hundreds of survivors was a very difficult task, but I was really excited to start. We decided to reach out to trafficking survivors, to broadcast their story at our event and inform our local community about modern day slavery, which many believe to be a nonexistent issue.

As one of the project managers, I realized the importance of multitasking and deadlines. As a dedicated project manager I felt that it was my responsibility as well to make sure my colleagues were on task. Reflecting on this experience later on taught me the importance of working efficiently and collaboratively.
