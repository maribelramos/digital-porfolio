Global to Local

Advisory Board: Global to Local

As a club we are dedicated in serving the community of Newburgh to better help it thrive as a whole. We plan to go to Quito, Ecuador in the summer of 2017 to pursue knowledge in service-learning in a foreign country that will provide us with the opportunity to identify and address the social problems and issues in our own community. Allowing us to attain a better perspective of our own local community to gain the necessary skills to enact change. We are currently involved in fundraising for the trip and strengthening our voice to the public.

Being apart of a program like this allows me to lend a hand to those who are working to improve life in their communities. It also allows me to interact with members of other local communities, to gain a better understanding of the culture, as well as the issues that affect that part of the world. I am looking forward of how this trip will better me as a student and as a member of my community.
